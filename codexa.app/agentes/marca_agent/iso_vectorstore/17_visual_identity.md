### Color Palette Structure

**Recommended Palette Size**: 3-5 colors

**Structure**:
1. **Primary** (60% usage): Main brand color, logo, headers
2. **Secondary** (30% usage): Accent, CTA buttons, highlights
3. **Neutral** (10% usage): Backgrounds, text, borders

**Optional**:
4. **Accent** (5% usage): Special elements, badges, alerts
5. **Complementary** (variations): Light/dark versions of primary/secondary

---

## ðŸ–‹ï¸ TYPOGRAPHY GUIDELINES

### Font Pairing Strategy

**2-Font System** (recommended for e-commerce):
- **Primary (Headers)**: Display/sans-serif for impact
- **Secondary (Body)**: Sans-serif for readability

**3-Font System** (optional for premium brands):
- **Primary (Headlines)**: Display/serif for elegance
- **Secondary (Subheadings)**: Sans-serif for clarity
- **Tertiary (Body)**: Sans-serif for readability

---

| Font Style | Personality | Best For | Brazilian Preference |
|-----------|-------------|----------|---------------------|

### Typography Scale

**Hierarchy** (responsive sizing):

```
H1 (Hero Headline): 48-72px desktop, 32-40px mobile
H2 (Section Headers): 36-48px desktop, 24-32px mobile
H3 (Subsections): 24-32px desktop, 20-24px mobile
Body (Paragraphs): 16-18px desktop, 14-16px mobile
Small (Captions): 12-14px desktop, 11-13px mobile
```

**Weight Hierarchy**:
- Headlines: Bold (700) or ExtraBold (800)
- Subheadings: SemiBold (600)
- Body: Regular (400)
- Captions: Regular (400) or Light (300)

### Photography Style Selection

**Choose ONE consistent style**:

**1. Lifestyle** (products in use)
- Best for: Fashion, home, wellness
- Mood: Aspirational, relatable
- Example: Model using product in natural setting

**2. Product-Focused** (clean, minimal)
- Best for: Tech, accessories, tools
- Mood: Professional, clear
- Example: White background, centered product

**3. Flat Lay** (overhead arrangement)
- Best for: Beauty, stationery, food
- Mood: Organized, aesthetic
- Example: Products arranged on textured surface

**4. Action** (product in motion)
- Best for: Sports, outdoor, active gear
- Mood: Dynamic, energetic
- Example: Product being used during activity

**5. Detail** (close-up, texture)
- Best for: Luxury, craft, premium
- Mood: Quality, craftsmanship
- Example: Macro shot of material/texture

## ðŸŒˆ ACCESSIBILITY (WCAG Compliance)

### Color Contrast Requirements

**WCAG AA** (minimum standard):
- Normal text: 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- Large text (18px+): 3:1 contrast ratio

**WCAG AAA** (enhanced):
- Normal text: 7:1 contrast ratio
- Large text: 4.5:1 contrast ratio

**Test Tools**:
- WebAIM Contrast Checker
- Color Safe (generates accessible palettes)
- Stark (Figma plugin)

---

### Color Blindness Considerations

**Types affecting 8% of males, 0.5% of females**:
- Deuteranopia (green-blind): Most common
- Protanopia (red-blind)
- Tritanopia (blue-blind)

**Solutions**:
- Don't rely on color alone (add icons, text, patterns)
- Use colorblind-safe palettes (blue + orange, purple + yellow)
- Test with simulators (Coblis, Color Oracle)
