## üéØ ARCHETYPE SELECTION GUIDE

### Step 1: Understand Product DNA
- What problem does it solve?
- Who is the ideal customer?
- What's the emotional benefit?

### Step 2: Analyze Market Position
- How do competitors position themselves?
- What archetype dominates the category?
- Where's the differentiation opportunity?

### Step 3: Match to Customer Values
- What does your customer aspire to?
- What are their core fears?
- What motivates their purchase?

### Step 4: Test Consistency
- Does this archetype fit product features?
- Can we deliver on the archetype promise?
- Will this resonate in Brazilian culture?

**Adapt Archetypes for Brazil**:

1. **Hero**: Less "individual achievement", more "team victory" (futebol culture)
2. **Caregiver**: Strong resonance (family-oriented culture)
3. **Explorer**: Popular with urban millennials seeking experiences
4. **Sage**: Respect for "doutores" and experts
5. **Innocent**: Works well with natural/organic products
6. **Magician**: Align with "jeitinho brasileiro" (creative problem-solving)

8. **Creator**: Growing with entrepreneurship culture
9. **Jester**: Very strong (Brazilian humor/joy culture)
10. **Everyman**: Powerful (egalitarian values)
11. **Lover**: Strong (passionate culture)
12. **Rebel**: Use carefully (avoid anti-authority extremes)
