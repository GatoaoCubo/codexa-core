**1. Funny ‚Üî Serious** (0-5 scale)

**3. Respectful ‚Üî Irreverent** (0-5 scale)

- 5 = Very irreverent (disruptive, challenger brands)

**4. Enthusiastic ‚Üî Matter-of-fact** (0-5 scale)
- 0 = Very matter-of-fact (technical, data-driven)
- 3-4 = Enthusiastic (most Brazilian brands - warm culture)
- 5 = Very enthusiastic (sales, promotions, youth)

## üéØ TAGLINE CREATION

### Tagline Formula Options

**1. Functional Benefit**
- Formula: [Action] + [Result]
- Example: "Venda mais, trabalhe menos"
- Best for: Utilitarian products, B2B

**2. Emotional Benefit**
- Formula: [Feeling] + [Transformation]
- Example: "Da ansiedade √† confian√ßa"
- Best for: Wellness, beauty, lifestyle

**3. Brand Promise**
- Formula: "We [verb] so you can [outcome]"
- Example: "Simplificamos a log√≠stica para voc√™ focar em vendas"
- Best for: Services, solutions

**4. Challenge/Provocation**
- Formula: Question or bold statement
- Example: "E se vender fosse f√°cil?"
- Best for: Disruptive brands, challengers

**5. Aspiration**
- Formula: Invite to ideal state
- Example: "Sua loja dos sonhos, hoje"
- Best for: Aspirational products

---

### Tagline Checklist

A great tagline should be:

- [ ] **Short** (3-6 words ideal, max 8 words)
- [ ] **Memorable** (easy to recall after 1 hearing)
- [ ] **Unique** (not generic, specific to your brand)
- [ ] **Benefit-focused** (what customer gains, not what you do)
- [ ] **Timeless** (not trendy, lasts 5+ years)
- [ ] **Brazilian PT** (sounds natural in Portuguese, not translated)
- [ ] **Ownable** (competitors can't use it)
- [ ] **Emotional** (creates feeling, not just information)

## üìä MESSAGE TESTING

### A/B Test Your Messages

**Test These Variables**:
1. **Tone** (formal vs casual)
2. **Focus** (benefit vs feature)
3. **Length** (short vs detailed)
4. **Emotion** (rational vs emotional)
5. **CTA** (direct vs soft)

**Example A/B Test**:
- **Version A** (rational): "Economize 30% em todos os produtos premium"
- **Version B** (emotional): "D√™ a voc√™ o que voc√™ merece. 30% off produtos premium"
- **Metric**: Click-through rate (CTR)

### Message Validation Questions

Before launching, ask:

- [ ] **Is it true?** (Can we deliver on this promise?)
- [ ] **Is it different?** (Do competitors say the same?)
- [ ] **Is it relevant?** (Do customers care?)
- [ ] **Is it clear?** (No jargon, no confusion?)
- [ ] **Is it Brazilian?** (Sounds natural in PT-BR, not translated?)
- [ ] **Is it consistent?** (Aligns with archetype + tone?)
- [ ] **Is it ownable?** (Unique to our brand?)
- [ ] **Is it memorable?** (Sticky, repeatable?)
