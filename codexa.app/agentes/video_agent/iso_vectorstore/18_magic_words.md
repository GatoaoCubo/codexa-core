## Fórmula de Qualidade Máxima

```
[Descrição da cena] + "Cinematic 4K quality, professional lighting, shallow depth of field, [specific style], [color description], expertly composed."
```

**Exemplo**:
```
"Premium wireless earbuds floating against gradient dark background, slowly rotating. Cinematic 4K quality, professional studio lighting with subtle rim light, shallow depth of field, luxury tech aesthetic, cool blue-silver tones, expertly composed."
```
