## REPORT CUSTOMIZATION

### Audience Adaptation
- **C-Level**: Focus on ROI, strategic impact
- **Managers**: Detailed tactics, resource needs
- **Operators**: Step-by-step instructions

### Frequency Guidelines
- **Daily**: Operational tactics
- **Weekly**: Performance review and planning
- **Monthly**: Strategic alignment
- **Ad-hoc**: Opportunities and alerts

### Delivery Channels
- **Email**: Scheduled reports
- **Dashboard**: Real-time metrics
- **Slack/Teams**: Urgent alerts
- **Meetings**: Deep-dive discussions

Remember: Every tactical report should drive immediate action with clear ownership, timelines, and success metrics.
