# Key Metrics and KPIs

**Categoria**: marketplace_optimization
**Qualidade**: 0.91/1.00
**Data**: 20251113

## Conteúdo

Track these metrics to optimize inventory:

- **Inventory Turnover**: (COGS / Average Inventory)
  - Higher = faster movement, lower carrying costs
  - Target: 5-10x annually for retail

- **Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)**: (365 / Inventory Turnover)
  - How many days inventory sits before selling
  - Lower is better (less cash tied up)

- **Stockout Rate**: Percentage of customer demand unfulfilled
  - Target: < 2%

- **Overstock Rate**: Percentage of excess inventory
  - High overstock = poor forecasting

- **Carrying Cost**: Annual cost to hold inventory
  - Warehouse space, insurance, spoilage, obsolescence
  - Typically 20-30% of inventory value
