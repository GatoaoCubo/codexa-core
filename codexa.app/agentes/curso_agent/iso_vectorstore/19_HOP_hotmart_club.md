## STEP 8: Configure Gotejamento (Drip Release)

**What is Gotejamento?**
- Progressive content unlock (weekly, bi-weekly, or by completion)
- **Why use**: Increases completion from 10-15% (full unlock) to 40-60% (drip)
- **Psychology**: Commitment, sunk cost, anticipation

**8.1 Choose Drip Strategy:**

**Strategy A: Time-Based Drip (Most Common)**
- Unlock modules on schedule (e.g., Week 1 = M1, Week 2 = M2)
- **Pro**: Simple, works for evergreen courses
- **Con**: Fast learners frustrated by waiting

**Strategy B: Completion-Based Drip**
- Unlock M2 only after M1 100% complete
- **Pro**: Rewards fast learners, ensures mastery
- **Con**: Slow learners fall behind cohort

**Strategy C: Hybrid**
- Unlock M2 after M1 OR 7 days, whichever comes first
- **Pro**: Best of both worlds
- **Con**: More complex setup

**Strategy D: Full Unlock (No Drip)**
- All content available immediately
- **Pro**: Freedom, fast learners love it
- **Con**: Low completion (10-15%), high refunds ("too easy")

**Recommendation**: Start with **Strategy A (Time-Based)** for evergreen, **Strategy C (Hybrid)** for cohort-based.

## üö® TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem 1: Students can't find lessons**
- **Cause**: Nested too deep, confusing taxonomy
- **Solution**: Simplify structure (max 4 levels), add M√≥dulo 0 with orientation

**Problem 2: Low completion rate (<20%)**
- **Cause**: No gotejamento, overwhelming content
- **Solution**: Enable time-based drip (weekly), shorten videos to 8-12 min

**Problem 3: Gotejamento not unlocking**
- **Cause**: Wrong date calculation, timezone issues
- **Solution**: Check drip settings, verify student's enrollment date, test with manual unlock

**Problem 4: Students complaining about locked content**
- **Cause**: Too aggressive drip (1 lesson per week), fast learners frustrated
- **Solution**: Switch to hybrid drip (completion OR time, whichever first)

**Problem 5: Attachments not downloading**
- **Cause**: File size >50MB, unsupported format
- **Solution**: Compress PDFs (use Smallpdf.com), convert files to supported formats
